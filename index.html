About TRINITY FORWARDERS

New Unity Clearing And Forwarding or TRINITY FORWARDERS in short, established in 2012, is a well-equipped Multi-model Transportation and Logistics Service provider in EAST AFRICA countries. We have completed three successful decades as a leader to the freight forwarders & clearing agents in the entire East africa .

We have a well-established organization with highly experienced staff around the globe . Moreover TRINITY FORWARDERS is working as an effective Purchaser and Transporter for various customers in different EAST AFRICA Countries .

The extensive network of clients in East africa, made TRINITY FORWARDERS a diversified Transport company through fulfilling their requirements effectively. With a tremendous experience in freight forwarding & clearing services, TRINITY FORWARDERS stands distinctly ahead in the industry with professional hands in all the aspects of freight transportation services.

Success Path

The list of our vivid customers include firms of chemical trade, ship spares and oil field equipment, Projects Cargos, BreakBulk cargo, Dangerous goods, electronics, automobile spare parts, perishables, foodstuff, machinery, manufactures and/or traders in garments & wearing apparel.

Today TRINITY FORWARDERS stands out as one of the top freight agents in Kenya and neighbouring EAST AFRICA countries. TRINITY FORWARDERS is managed by a multi –cultural group of professionals, having immense experience in the field, Eveready for providing top notch service and business run.
